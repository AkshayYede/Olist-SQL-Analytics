## 1. Overall Business Scale

* Total orders analyzed: **98,666**
* Total revenue (items + freight): **15,843,553.24**

**Insight:**
The platform operates at mid-to-large e-commerce scale with substantial transaction volume.

## 2. Revenue Trend Analysis

* Revenue shows steady growth from early 2017
* Peak revenue observed in **late 2017 and early 2018**
* Minimal revenue in late 2016 aligns with early platform activity

**Insight:**
Business experienced strong growth momentum through 2017, indicating successful customer and seller adoption.

## 3. Product Category Performance

Top revenue-generating categories:

1. Health & Beauty
2. Watches & Gifts
3. Bed Bath & Table
4. Sports & Leisure
5. Computers & Accessories

**Insight:**
A small number of categories drive a large share of total revenue, suggesting concentration in lifestyle and consumer goods.

## 4. Seller Performance Distribution

* Top sellers generate disproportionately higher revenue
* Majority of top-performing sellers are located in **São Paulo (SP)**

**Insight:**
Seller performance is highly skewed, and geographic concentration suggests regional advantages in logistics or demand.

---

## 5. Delivery Performance Analysis

* Average delivery time for delivered orders: **~12.5 days**
* Canceled orders exhibit significantly higher delivery durations

**Insight:**
Delivery delays are strongly associated with order cancellations and operational inefficiencies.

---

## 6. Delivery Time vs Customer Satisfaction

* Average delivery days decrease as review score increases
* 5-star reviews correspond to the fastest deliveries
* 1–2 star reviews show the longest delivery times

**Insight:**
Delivery speed is a key driver of customer satisfaction and review outcomes.

---

## 7. Customer Ordering Behavior

* Presence of repeat customers with **5–17 orders**
* Majority of customers place a single order

**Insight:**
While most customers are one-time buyers, a smaller segment demonstrates strong repeat behavior, indicating potential for retention strategies.

---

## 8. Overall Assessment

* Revenue growth, category concentration, and seller skew are clearly observable
* Logistics performance directly impacts customer satisfaction
* Customer repeat behavior exists but is limited to a small segment

**Final Verdict:**
The analytics model successfully surfaces actionable business insights related to growth, operations, seller performance, and customer behavior.
